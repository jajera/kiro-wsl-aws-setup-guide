## WSL Targets

Open the Remote Explorer (monitor icon) → **WSL Targets** → you should see **docker-desktop** and **Ubuntu-26.04**:

![Kiro WSL Targets in Remote Explorer](/kiro-wsl-aws-setup-guide/images/kiro-wsl-targets.png)

## Connect and Open Terminal

Connect to **Ubuntu-26.04** → `` Ctrl+` `` → confirm status bar shows **WSL: Ubuntu-26.04** and bash prompt `<username>@<hostname>:~$`:

![Kiro WSL connected with bash terminal](/kiro-wsl-aws-setup-guide/images/kiro-wsl-terminal.png)
